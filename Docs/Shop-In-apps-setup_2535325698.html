Blast Royale : Shop In-apps setup

Created by Nik Denissov on Nov 25, 2024

We have to setup in three places:

  1. Google console

  2. Apple console

  3. Playfab

Purchases should be in the format: com.firstlight.blastroyale.<what the purchase is about>

For example: com.firstlight.blastroyale.premiumpass

In both Apple and Google we use default pricing without modifying any regional changes.

Apple

Go to in-app purchases

You can add new ones here

Apple requires screenshot from device (or at least with the resolution of one of their devices) before you can submit for review. Review means the purchase should be in a testfligh build.
